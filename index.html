Vikram Batra

Captain Vikram Batra PVC (9 September 1974 – 7 July 1999) was an officer of the Indian Army.

BIBLIOGRAPHY

Batra was born on 9 September 1974, in a small town in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh. He was the third child of Girdhari Lal Batra, a government school principal, and Kamal Kanta Batra, a school teacher.
